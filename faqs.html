Any Questions?

Why would a student apply to LearnAbyte?

LearnAbyte is a fast-paced, immersive, hybrid education experience. It provides both online and offline learning experience from experts with a self paced learning process.

What skills or experience do a child develop after starting to learn coding?

t's important for a child to have a solid foundation of basic math skills and understanding of quadratic and linear equations may be beneficial as your problem-solving skills will help you locate the issue and find a way to resolve it. These skills are developed through coding.

Can every kid learn Coding?

Whether you are a student interested in IT careers, wanting to explore more with a coding qualification or just fascinated by the world of tech and coding courses, LearnAbyte offers a wide range of online coding courses both online and offline that make coding accessible to kids and young teenagers. LearnAbyte allows kids to grow interest in coding and how to navigate them in the best way.

What will you learn in a coding course for kids?

LearnAbyte coding courses are oriented towards coding and programming and here you will learn the essentials of coding like logic, structure & sequence, how to create websites, animations & apps. You will be also taught how to manipulate data using programming languages to be a problem solver.

What is the process of resolving doubts

When a student has a doubt then he/she would raise a “Help Request”. This “Help Request” would be circulated within the group first and the mentor is also alerted with regards to this “Help Request”. The doubt can then be either clarified via the peer group or by the mentor via text or video call.

What if I miss a lecture?

No worries, we got you covered. All lectures are recorded and you will have a lifetime access to the recordings after the lecture so you can cope up with the matter in your own pace.
