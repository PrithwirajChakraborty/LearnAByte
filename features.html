Hybrid Learning

It requires the physical presence of both teacher and learner while providing the learner some control over time and pace. Physical Problem Solving

Physical problem solving is converting knowledge into behavior to achieve a goal and it takes skill and intuition to use such relations to solve physical problems. To become adept at problem solving, you have to practice, and you have to pay attention to what you do. Studies of good problem solvers find that such people have certain habits that contribute to their success even you can study these habits, and with practice you can make them your own.

Digital Tech Support

Digital tech support is a support by a company to oversee and maintain their computer hardware and software systems. Their skills are an asset to the company because they assist in resolving technical issues concerning clients or company software infrastructure and so on.

Dynamic Evaluation

The term dynamic evaluation refers to an assessment by an active teaching process of a child's perception, learning, thinking, and problem solving. The process aims at modifying an individual's cognitive functioning and observing subsequent changes in learning and problem-solving patterns within the testing situation.
